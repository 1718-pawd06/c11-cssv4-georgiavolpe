What is art? How should someone categorize it?

When people think of art, they may imagine paintings or drawings, but that is not always the case. So, what defines art? "Art" can range from advertisements, the strum of a guitar, the way someone walks, the directions the grass grows, etc!

Traditional art is probably what most people think of when they hear the word "art." The term "traditional" implies sculptures, portraits, and other man-made pieces. Since technology continues to develop over the years, there is also another category of art that has emerged called "digital" art. This could be used to make commercials or advertisements, but many just use it to create what they want!

Julia Razumova is a popular artist on social media that specializes in drawing digital portraits. Sometimes, people send her pictures of themselves and ask for her to illustrate them. You can see some of Julia's art here.

Emilio Villalba is an artist in New York who paints portraits in a way not many would expect. "I've always been fascinated by the mess," he states. Emilio is very well known for his unique and abstract depiction of what, or who, people really are. You can see more of his art on his website here.
